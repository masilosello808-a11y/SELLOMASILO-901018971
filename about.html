About Us

Golden Path Mechanical Services was founded by Sello MasiloSince its establishment, the company has been committed to providing professional, reliable, and affordable mechanical services to the local community.

Mission

To deliver quality automotive services, to ensure that every vehicle we service operates safely and efficiently

Vision

To be the leading mechanical service provider in Lesotho and across Africa.

Our Values

  • Professionalism: We handle every vehicle with care and expertise.
  • Integrity: Honest advice and transparent pricing are our priorities.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Every service is focused on exceeding expectations.
  • Community Focus: We aim to support the local Leribe community and promote safety.

Meet Our Founder

Sello Masilo has over 10 years of experience in automotive services and is passionate about delivering dependable mechanical solutions to every client.
